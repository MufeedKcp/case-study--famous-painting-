## Overview

This project analyzes a dataset of famous paintings to uncover insights about:

* Artists and their popularity
* Painting styles and trends
* Museum distributions
* Pricing patterns
* Subject popularity
* Country-level comparisons

The goal was to simulate how SQL is used in **real business analytics scenarios**.

---

## Key Business Questions Answered

This case study answers questions such as:

1. Which paintings are not displayed in any museum?
2. Are there museums without any paintings?
3. How many paintings are priced higher than their regular price?
4. Which paintings have discounts greater than 45%?
5. What is the most expensive canvas size?
6. How to remove duplicate records from multiple tables?
7. What are the top 10 most popular painting subjects?
8. Which museums are open on both Sunday and Monday?
9. What are the top 5 most popular museums?
10. Who are the top 5 most popular artists?
11. Where is the most expensive painting displayed?
12. What are the 3 most and least popular painting styles?
13. Which country has the 5th highest number of paintings?
14. Which artist has the most portrait paintings outside the USA?
15. Which artists have paintings displayed in multiple countries?
16. Which museum has the highest number of paintings of the most popular style?

---

## Key Insights

* Identified the most and least popular painting styles
* Found the top artists based on number of artworks
* Analyzed museum popularity by total paintings
* Discovered pricing anomalies and heavy discounts
* Analyzed global distribution of artworks
* Cleaned duplicate records using window functions
